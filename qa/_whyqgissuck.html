Why QGIS suck

Abstract

I'm a huge fan of QGIS, but it has a long way ahead of becoming truly high performance, user-friendly desktop GIS. And of course this is what I personally expect desktop GIS to be. Bear in mind, that you might be very different person and this essay will be totally irrelevant to you.

The goal of this article is not to diminish this opensource GIS tool, but on exact opposite - steer the community and help pinpoint critical disadvantages. Rage is good, let it out!
