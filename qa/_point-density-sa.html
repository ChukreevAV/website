Алгоритмы расчета плотности точек в Arcview GIS/Spatial Analyst

Краткая аннотация

Simple - Density is calculated for each cell by summing the value found in Population Field for each point found in the Search Radius and dividing by the area of the circle in Area Units.

Kernel - Density is calculated the same way as with Simple, except the value found in Population Field is distributed around each point. The result is a smoother looking output.
