Geographic Information Systems and Remote Sensing

GIS-Lab is the informal GIS/RS community created in 2002. It is one of the few non-profit online communities in this field in Russian that operates one of the most complete and active practical resources devoted to the field. This website is a collection of user-written articles which present solutions to the most common problems and tasks. Most of the materials are in Russian, but we occasionally translate some of our articles in English.

"No matter how much you know about a subject, there's always someone else who knows more; including yourself, one week later."

To find an answer to your question start from Articles, you can also try to get help on our Forum.

GIS-Lab will be glad to help with GIS/RS related questions. Just send us a note!
