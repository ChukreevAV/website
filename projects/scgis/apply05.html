Society for Conservation GIS - Online Application Form

To submit your SCGIS scholarship application, please fill the form below. When you hit the button 'Send' at the bottom of the form, a standard SCGIS application will be automatically generated and emailed to the SCGIS International Committee and to the address that you indicate in the field 'Your direct email address' (this field MUST be filled). We will use this address to get back to you shortly after receiving your application, and to communicate in the future.

Some fields are supplied with comments, however, before filling and submitting the form please read the instructions completely.

The information that you provide here will not be distributed in any way. However, if you are granted a scholarship, non-confidential information may be posted on the Society for Conservation GIS website, or reprinted in the SCGIS newsletter.

Please note that the deadline for application submittions has been extended to February 15th, 2005.
